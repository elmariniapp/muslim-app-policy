Privacy Policy – Muslim App

Last updated: 2025-12-05

Muslim App fully respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights under GDPR and Google Play requirements.

1. Information We Collect

Muslim App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data on external servers. All data remains only on your device.

Local Data (stored on your device only)

  • App settings (theme, language, font size)
  • Prayer calculation method
  • Reading progress (surahs read, last position)
  • Favorites & saved duas
  • User onboarding choices

Optional Data (only if enabled)

  • Approximate location (Qibla, nearby mosques)
  • Notifications (prayer reminders)

2. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used only to provide core app features: prayer times, Qibla direction, reading progress, and notifications.

3. Data Sharing

Muslim App does not share any data with third parties.

4. App Permissions

Location: used only for prayer times, Qibla, and mosques. Never stored nor transmitted.

Notifications: used only for reminders.

5. Data Security

All data stays locally stored on your device.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

  • Right of access
  • Right to delete
  • Right to withdraw consent

7. Children’s Privacy

No data is collected from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

Updates will be displayed within the app.
